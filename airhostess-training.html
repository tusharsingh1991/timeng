About this Course

YOUR DREAM, OUR PRIORITY! Taking that first step makes it a reality and before you realize you are already flying.

Workplace for active, curious, communicative, and self-confident people exists not only on the ground! At least one description you can assign for yourself? Then you are destined to take off into the sky! The Initial Cabin Crew Training Course is the first and essential step towards the flight attendant career. The training is dedicated to cover all matters concerning specific cabin crew member proficiency requirements and should be followed by the Conversion Training Course for the specific aircraft type.

As the demand for air travel grows, the aviation industry believes in ensuring a safe journey to every one of its passengers. YOU could be a part of the aviation family fulfilling this awesome vision and mission.

    What you'll learn in this course?

  • English communication
  • Introduction to Aviation Industry
  • Personal Grooming and Presentation 
  • Poise
  • Interview skills
  • Aircraft familiarisation
  • Emergency situation handling
  • Basic first aid
  • Personality Development 
  • Passenger psychology 
  • Interview preparation
